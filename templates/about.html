LMI Health

At LMI Health, we address the root causes of illness with sustainable lifestyle changes, focusing on nutrition, activity, stress management, and social connections to prevent and reverse chronic diseases. We prioritize inclusivity and equity, ensuring everyone has access to optimal health. We empower individuals and communities through education, advocacy, and personalized care to take transformative steps toward better health.

Vision

To become the leading provider of evidence-based Lifestyle Medicine services in the region, promoting holistic health practices as the foundation of personal and collective well-being, and ultimately fostering the creation of a “Blue Zone” in Zambia.

Mission

To demonstrate the transformative power of lifestyle interventions to a society often reliant on alternative/conventional medicine by helping people with chronic conditions reverse their effects and potentially achieve remission. To promote holistic health and well-being by becoming a hub for training healthcare professionals in lifestyle medicine and educating the community on healthy living practices. Additionally, we seek to transform the sector of occupational health services, making specific occupational lifestyles safer through policy enhancements and preventive approaches.

At Lifestyle Medicine Institute (LMI), we believe that true health and well-being are achieved by addressing the root causes of illness and promoting sustainable lifestyle changes. Our approach is grounded in evidence-based lifestyle medicine, emphasizing the critical role of nutrition, physical activity, stress management, and social connections in preventing and reversing chronic diseases. We focus on addressing the root causes of illness rather than just relying on medication, which often comes with side effects, increased costs, and other complications.

We recognize the importance of intersectionality, understanding that health disparities often arise from complex interplays of socioeconomic factors, race, gender, and access to resources. Our programs are designed to be inclusive and equitable, ensuring that all individuals, regardless of their background, have the opportunity to achieve optimal health.

In alignment with the principles of the American College of Lifestyle Medicine, our philosophy is to empower individuals and communities through education, advocacy, and personalized care. At LMI, we adhere to the belief that “you can never achieve higher than your aim.” Inspired by this, we encourage our tribe to continuously take small, transformative steps that lure dreams into experience.

Our Story

Discover the journey of passion, perseverance, and purpose behind the institute.

Early Inspiration

Dr. Kaite’s journey began long before he stepped into the bustling Emergency Department of a tertiary-level hospital in Zambia. Growing up in the Seventh-day Adventist Church, he was immersed in a community that championed the benefits of a healthy lifestyle.

A Call for Change

Treating emergencies felt like endlessly mopping a flooded floor without addressing the source of the leak. Determined to make a profound difference, Dr. Kaite pursued a Master of Social and Public Policy.

The Lifestyle Medicine Discovery

Lifestyle Medicine illuminated the path he had been searching for—combining medical expertise with preventive care. This was the turning point in his mission to fight chronic diseases at their root.

A Vision Realized

Together with his father, John Kaite, the institute was founded to champion holistic health. Their journey serves as an inspiring narrative of hope, vision, and transformation.
